Who We Are

National Rigging & Crane Training LLC

National Rigging and Crane Training LLC provides professional training for crane operators, riggers, signalpersons, inspectors, maintenance teams, and industrial organizations that need practical, standards-based instruction.

NRCT focuses on helping companies and individuals improve safety, strengthen jobsite knowledge, and better understand the equipment, inspection requirements, and lifting practices used in industrial environments.

The company’s training approach combines technical knowledge with practical application, giving students instruction that is clear, relevant, and grounded in real industry experience.

About Peter Cooke

Train With the Expert Behind the Standards

Peter Cooke brings more than two decades of experience in engineering, crane and rigging training, equipment design, certification development, and industry standards.

Peter holds a degree in Industrial Engineering and spent more than 20 years with Columbus McKinnon Corporation, where he designed below-the-hook lifting devices, specialty forged rigging equipment, and slings.

During his time as Corporate Training Manager, Peter developed certification programs for rigging, hoist and crane operations, hoist and crane repair, and hoist and crane inspections.

Peter’s background gives NRCT a unique advantage: training that is backed by technical knowledge, real-world equipment experience, certification development, and industry committee involvement.
